The title of my project

Character Animation

Overview

For this project, I created a character animation. I originally completed this project in 2019. The software used for this project was Adobe Character Animate. The videos consist of 2D motion characters walking and talking. For the audio, I made use of random .wav files I found online that correspond to natural sounds (eg. walking steps).

I am currently working with my team on improving the videos, such as adding more characters, creating action and developing a storyline. This enabled us switch to more powerful tools such as Blender. Once ready, they will be posted soon!

Enjoy the videos!
