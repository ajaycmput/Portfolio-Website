Character Design

a Journey

Overview

For this project, I created a type of character animation, which was completed in 2019. The software used for this project was Adobe Character Animate. The videos consist of 2D motion characters walking and talking. For the audio, I made use of random .wav files I found online that correspond to "man yelling" , "footsteps" etc.

I am currently working with my team on improving the videos, such as adding more characters, creating action and developing a storyline. This enabled us switch to more powerful animation tools such as Blender. Once ready, they will be posted soon!

Enjoy the videos!
