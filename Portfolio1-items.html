Project Overview



Reporter

At Ansys, I initially worked on a project called "The Reporter". This automated tool streamlines the process of gathering and summarizing test results from our company servers. Every day, it pulls essential data attributes, organizes them into a comprehensive report, and sends them to designated recipients.



QA-lytics

Building on the data extraction groundwork laid by The Reporter, I developed QA-lytics, a dash app aimed at providing deeper insights. The app features a user-friendly GUI for analyzing various types of visual data, from graphs to pivot tables, tailored to the needs of our developers.

It is compatible with powerful tools like Power BI and includes advanced features such as anomaly detection and context-sensitive alerts for test results. This ensures not only the integrity of the data but also the coherence of the database and system.
