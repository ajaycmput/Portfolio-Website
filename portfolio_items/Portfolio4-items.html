Robot Design

From Concept to Creation

Overview

In 2018, I was part of a team that designed and modeled a robotic car as part of a robotics contest. The car was equipped with sensors to facilitate movement and was preprogrammed to follow a specific set of instructions.

This project required strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work effectively in a team environment. It also allowed me to gain valuable experience in the field of robotics and contribute to the advancement of technology.

This project has the potential to serve as a foundation for the development of future robotic cars and other innovative technologies. I am excited to continue exploring the field of robotics and finding new ways to contribute to its advancement. Thank you for visiting and I hope you find the information on my website informative and engaging.
