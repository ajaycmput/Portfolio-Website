Electronic Payment System

Online Payment

Overview

I developed an Electronic Payment System called Ajay's Paybud as a mobile application project. This payment system enables users to make payments to any client and was designed with the purpose of simplifying the payment process for students in remote areas. It is intended to work in conjunction with a Travel Advisor app that is currently in progress.

The Payment System was implemented using Flutter SDK and was tested using an Android emulator. Work on an iOS emulator is ongoing.

Please note that new features are being added and tested on a regular basis, and the project is still in the process of expansion. Once complete, the app will be available for free download on the Google Play and App Store (iOS).

This project showcases my skills in mobile app development and the ability to create a functional and user-friendly product. I am excited to continue exploring the field of app development and finding new ways to apply my skills and knowledge. Thank you for visiting and I hope you find the information on my website informative and engaging.
