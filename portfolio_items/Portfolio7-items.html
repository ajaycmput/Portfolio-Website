Character Design

a Journey

Overview

In 2019, I completed a project in character animation using Adobe Character Animate. The project consisted of 2D motion videos featuring characters walking and talking, with audio files sourced from online sources.

I am currently working with a team to improve the videos by adding more characters, incorporating action, and developing a storyline. In order to do so, we have switched to using more powerful animation tools such as Blender. The updated videos will be available on my website once they are ready.

This project showcases my skills in character animation and the ability to use animation software to create engaging content. I am excited to continue exploring the field of animation and finding new ways to apply my skills and knowledge. Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy the videos.
